# Chapter 1 Progression (Duolingo-style)
Total lessons: 12 lessons
---
Stages:
Stage 1 — inherent vowel reading
Stage 2 — vowel sign introduction
Stage 3 — reading expansion
Stage 4 — conjunct concept

---
# STAGE 1 — Inherent Vowel Reading
Explain implicitly:
Consonants contain vowel অ

Example:
ক = kô
জ = jô

---

# Lesson 1 — First Word

Letters introduced: আ, ম.
Word: আম (am - mango).
Goal: Read first Bengali word

Concept:
Letter recognition
Sound association

---
# Lesson 2 — First Consonant Expansion

New letters: ক, ল.
Word: কলম (kolom - pen).
Goal: Read 3-letter word

Concept:
Consonant + inherent vowel

---
# Lesson 3 — More Consonants
New letters: জ.
Word: জল (jol - water).
Goal: Recognize syllable pattern

---
# Lesson 4 — Expand Vocabulary
New letters: ঘ.
Word: ঘর (ghor - house).
Goal: Increase decoding speed

---
# Lesson 5 — Nasal Consonant
New letters: ন.
Words: বন (bon - forest), নগর (nogor - city).

Also introduce: গ.
Goal: Recognize longer words

---
# Lesson 6 — Remaining Word
New letters: ফ.
Word: ফল (phol - fruit).
Goal: Fluent reading of inherent vowel words

Observation:
At this point the learner can read:
* আম
* কলম
* জল
* ঘর
* বন
* ফল
* নগর

---
# STAGE 2 — Vowel Sign Introduction

Now explain explicitly:

Consonant contains vowel অ
Example:
ক = kô

Then introduce vowel signs.

---
# Lesson 7 — First Vowel Sign
Introduce: া.
Explain: ক + া = কা.
Words: মা.
Goal: Understand vowel modification

---
# Lesson 8 — Use Vowel Sign in Familiar Letters
Words: না.
Letters already known: ন + া.
Goal: Practice vowel sign reading.

---
# Lesson 9 — First Multi-Syllable Kar Word
Word: নাম.
Goal: Combine kar with learned words.

---
# Lesson 10 — Introduce New Vowel Sign
Introduce: ি.
Explain: ক + ি = কি.
Word: আমি.
Goal: Understand vowel placement

---
# STAGE 3 — Reading Integration
Now learners know:
inherent vowel: া,  ি.
Practice reading: আমি নাম লিখি.
But we avoid new vocabulary.
Focus on:
* আমি
* নাম
* আম

---

# STAGE 4 — Conjunct Concept
Introduce: Consonant clusters.
Example: ন + দ = ন্দ.
Word বন্ধু.
Goal: Recognize conjunct structure.

---
# Final Reading
Simple text:
* আমি কলম লিখি।
* আমার নাম আছে।

---
# Alphabet Introduced in Chapter
Vowels: অ, আ, ই.
Consonants: ম, ক, ল, জ, ঘ, ন, গ, ফ, ব, র.
Vowel signs: া, ি.
Conjunct: ন্দ.

# Chapter 1 Progression (Duolingo-style)
Total lessons: 12 lessons
---
Stages:
Stage 1 — inherent vowel reading
Stage 2 — vowel sign introduction
Stage 3 — reading expansion
Stage 4 — conjunct concept

-------------------------------------
# STAGE 1 — Inherent Vowel Reading
* Learn first Bengali words.
* Learn multiple inherent vowels and consonants.

### Vocabulary:
* আম (am - mango)
* কলম (kolom - pen)
* বই (boii - book)
* লবণ (lobon - salt)
* ঘর (ghor - house)
* বন (bon - forest)
* ফল (phol - fruit)
-------------------------------------

# Lesson 1 — First Word

Letters introduced: আ (a), ম (mo).
Word: আম (am - mango).
Goal: Read first Bengali word

Concept:
Letter recognition
Sound association

---
# Lesson 2 — First Consonant Expansion

New letters: ক (ko), ল (lo).
Word: কলম (kolom - pen).
Goal: Read 3-letter word.

Concept: Consonant + inherent vowel.

---
# Lesson 3 — More Consonants
New letters: ব (bo), ই (i).
Word: বই (boii - book).

Phrase: বই (boii), কলম (kolom).

Goal: Recognize syllable pattern.

---
# Lesson 4 — Expand Vocabulary
New letters: ঘ (gho).
Word: ঘর (ghor - house).
Goal: Increase decoding speed

---
# Lesson 5 — Similar Consonants
New letters: ন (no), ণ (no).
Words: বন (bon - forest), লবণ (lobon - salt),

Goal: Recognize longer words

---
# Lesson 6 — Remaining Word
New letters: ফ (pho).
Word: ফল (phol - fruit).
Goal: Fluent reading of inherent vowel words



-------------------------------------
# STAGE 2 — Vowel Sign Introduction

* Introduction of Vowel Signs.

### Vocabulary
* মা (ma - mother)
* বাবা (baba - father)
* চা (cha - tea)
* কমলা (komola - orange)
* কলা (kola - banana).
* ভাত (bhat - rice)

* কফি (kofi - coffee)
* চিনি (chini - sugar).
* পানি (pani - water)
* কাপ (kap - cup).
* চাবি (chabi - key)

-------------------------------------

# Lesson 7 — First Vowel Sign
Introduce: া (a), চ (cho).
Explain: ম (mo) + া (a) = মা (ma).
Words: মা (ma - mother), বাবা (baba - father), চা (cha - tea).
Goal: Understand vowel modification.

---
# Lesson 8 — Use Vowel Sign in Familiar Letters
Words: কমলা (komola - orange), কলা (kola - banana).
Letters already known: ন + া.
Goal: Practice vowel sign reading.

---
# Lesson 9 — Practice kar words
Introduce: ভ (bho), ত (to).
Word: ভাত (bhat - rice).
Goal: Combine kar with learned words.

---
# Lesson 10 — Introduce New Vowel Sign
Introduce: ি.
Word: কফি (kophi - coffee), চিনি (chini - sugar).
Goal: Understand vowel placement

---
# Lesson 11 - Practice more kar words
Introduce: প (po). 
Word: পানি (pani - water), কাপ (kap - cup), চাবি (chabi - key).

-------------------------------------
# STAGE 3 — Reading Integration

What the learner already has contact at this point:
* Inherent vowels: আ (a), ই (i).
* Consonants: ম (mo), ক (ko), ল (lo), ব (bo), ণ (no), ন (no), ঘ (gho), র (ro), ফ (pho), চ (cho), ভ (bho), ত (to), প (po).
* Vowel signs: া (a),  ি (i).

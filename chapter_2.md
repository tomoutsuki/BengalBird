# Chapter 2 — Vowel Expansion & Simple Sentences
**Total lessons:** 14 lessons

Goal of chapter:

* Learn remaining common vowel signs
* Read natural short sentences
* Introduce subject–object–verb order
* Introduce common everyday words

-------------------------------------

# STAGE 4 — New Vowel Signs
Expand to **core vowel system**.
Introduce **one vowel sign per lesson**.

### Words: 

* মেঘ (megh - cloud)
* কেক (kek - cake)
* আপেল (apel - apple)

* বোতল (botl - bottle)
* ভালো (bhalo - good)
* টমেটো (tometo - tomato)

* আলো (alo - light)
* সিনেমা (sinema - cinema)
* মোবাইল (mobail - mobile)

-------------------------------------

## Lesson 1 — Vowel Sign ে

Introduce: ে.
Words: মেঘ (megh - cloud), কেক (kek - cake), আপেল (apel - apple).
Goal: Learn **left-position vowel sign**.

---
## Lesson 2 — Vowel Sign ো
Introduce: ো (o), ট (to).
Words: বোতল (botl - bottle), ভালো (bhalo - good), টমেটো (tometo - tomato).
Goal: Recognize **two-part vowel sign**.

---
## Lesson 3 — Practice more words
Words: আলো (alo - light), সিনেমা (sinema - cinema), মোবাইল (mobail - mobile).
Goal: increase vocabulary, practice reading and writing.

## Lesson 4 — Vowel Sign ু

Introduce: ু (u)
Words: ফুল (phul - flower), কুকুর (kukur - dog), লেবু (lebu - lemon).
Goal: Recognize **bottom vowel sign**.

## Lesson 5 - Consonant ড় (ro)

Introduce: ড় (ro), গ (go).
Words: বিড়াল (biral - cat), বাড়ি (bari - house), গাড়ি (gari - car).
Goal: Learn words with ড় (ro), without deepening nukta concept.
